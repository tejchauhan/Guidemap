EDISON BLOCK




This block is mainly for ece and cse students . Chitkara university is best reputed in electronics and communication engineering department and communication engineering in Punjab . Edison block consists of 4 floor . It consists of high talented staff for students . This block consists of many high technology computer and electrical labs to fully recognise the high quality skills of students .

Ground floor - It consists of Digital Electronic and Logic Design lab . Where we can design and analyse the electrical aspects . It also consists of Basic of Electronic and Electrical Engineering lab , project lab and Design and Analysis of Electronics lab . Inlingua department is there in this floor which is the international school of languages has faculty which teach about particular accents like rio De janerio , los angeles , chicago and many more .
First floor - It consists of 4 lecture halls for students of B.E. course , Curie hall , office of brand communication and Office of Student Affairs dept where all the student affairs are acknowledge for various events of the university .
Second Floor - It consists of Mooc hall , digital electronics lab . It also consists of Office of Information Technology , faculty rooms and lecture halls for electronics and electrical engineering .
Third Floor - It includes application lab , VLSI lab , Digatal signal lab . It has one server room where control of all computer labs is maintained of edison , office of information technology .
