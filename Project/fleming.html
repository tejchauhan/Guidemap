FLEMING BLOCK




Chitkara university provides state-of-the-art building block.Fleming block is one of them. it is situated behind the turing block.This block consists of five floors .

Ground floor - It consists of learning Resource centre .
First Floor - It consists of pharmacognosy , Remedial biology laboratory . It also has one pharma laboratoryand instrumental rooms for pharmacy students . It includes Nanometrials Research lab and Machine Rooms which is highly equipped with advance tools . These all labs play an important role in their learning experience . It also consists of placement cell with high build seminar halls where companies come for placements .
Second floor - It consists of Herbal product tesling and standardlisation lab . It also includes pharmaceutical analysis lab , pharmaceutical chemistry lab , pharmacology lab and Aphe lab . It has professor rooms , computer labs and one lecture hall .
Third floor - It includes two research labs with highly equipped tools , four lecture halls , faculty rooms and office of HOD of pharmacy department .
Fourth floor - It is for business school department . It has five lecture halls , faculty rooms and tutorial rooms it has Warren Buffet Hall and office of assistant dean and assistant professors .
Fifth floor - It has thirteen lecture halls two faculty rooms . In this floor classes of BBA and B.COM course students take place .
