TURING BLOCK






Turing block is one of the highly structured building block of chitkara university it act as a common ground for students , faculty and corporative personalities for regular interfaces , conferences and other events . It provide world class education for all-round development of students . It provides the most conductive atmosphere for dynamic and focussed studies . This block is mainly for B.E. course students . It consists of six floors .

Ground floor - It consists of Dean office of Applied Science , faculty room , Maker's lab , chemistry lab , physics lab , BEE lab and three lecture halls .
First floor - It consists of Placement cell , Sun hall ,computer lab , three lecture halls and faculty room .
Second floor - It consists of six lecture halls, a tutorial room , three computer labs (access is denied through Turing ) and faculty room .
Third floor - It consists of library , tutorial rooms , faculty rooms and eleven lecture halls .
Fourth floor - It consists of Basic Electronic lab , Office of CSE department , nine lecture halls and faculty room .
Fifth floor - It consists of ED hall , four lecture halls , five computer labs and faculty rooms .
This block provides all the facilities required to meet the academic requirements as per the university curriculum .
