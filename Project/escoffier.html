ESCOFFIER BLOCK




Chitkara university has a building dedicated for hotel management and hospitality students . It is situated behind Square One . This block consists of two buildings merged together having four floors each .

I

Ground floor - It consists of restaurant and tuc shop on the outside.
First Floor - It consists of knowledge center and office of Director of Chitkara college of Hotel Management and Catering .
Second floor - It consists Confectionery labs .
Third floor - It consists of locker rooms , audio visual room and housekeeping labs .
Fourth floor - It consists of BTK 1 , BTK 2 and restaurant .

II

Ground floor - It consists of advanced training kitchen .
First Floor - It consists of three guest rooms , chef office , kitchen of the world , culinary studio and bakery and confectionery .
Second floor - It consists of front office lab , five lecture halls and faculty room .
Third floor - It consists of five lecture halls .
Fourth floor - It consists of boys hostel .
