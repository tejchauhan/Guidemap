TESLA BLOCK




Chitkara university provides state-of-the-art building block for Mechanical engineering , polytechnic , megatronics , LEET students . It is situated behind the Exploretorium . This block consists of four floors .

Ground floor - It consists of mechanical workshop , plumbering workshop and electronic workshop .
First Floor - It consists of group discussion room , five lecture halls , basic automation lab , interview room , chemistry lab and faculty room .
Second floor - It consists of EG hall , basic automation lab , classrooms and faculty rooms .
Third floor - It includes electronic lab , EG hall , canteen ,classrooms for Leet students and faculty rooms .
Fourth floor - It consists of dean office , office of assistant professors and professors , AEG lab and seven lecture halls .
