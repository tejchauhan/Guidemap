LE CORBUSIER BLOCK




This block is for chitkara school of planning and architecture students . It is one of the well maintained building of chitkara university where students are provided with knowledge of planning , designing and constructing buildings . It has three floors . This block is located by the side of babbage block .

Ground Floor - It consists of Pierre Jeanneret hall , lecture hall ,computer lab , deputy dean office , dean office of research and publication , suprintendent office , dean office of CSPA ( chitkara school of planning and architecture ) . It also has an open stage on the back side along with tuc shop and canteen .
First Floor - It consists of knowledge centre , seven faculty rooms , UPS room , exam room , three studios .
Second Floor - It has two lecture halls , six studios , staff room and office of deputy dean of student affairs and exchange program .
