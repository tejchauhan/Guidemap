GALILEO BLOCK




This block is mainly for bsc and pharmacy students . It is highly equipped with tools and technology . It has five floors This block is located in front of Beta-Ground . It is facilated with professional staff providing the following facilities for research in pharmaceutical science .
◦  Analysis of samples for infrared and UV Data
◦  HPLC Analysis
◦  Formulation studies
◦  Analytic method Development
◦  Clinical Evaluation
Ground Floor - It consists of Einstein hall , Principal office of pharmacy department , pre-clinical lab and faculty lounge .
First Floor - It consists of OBG lab , Nursing Foundation lab , Nursing Foundation lab ,Nutrition lab and one seminar room .
Second Floor - It has lecture Halls for Pharmacy and Bsc department , AV lab , Faculty rooms and one Common Room for girls .
Third Floor - It includes Community Health Nursing Lab , Record Room , class rooms , faculty room , a common room for boys .
Fourth Floor - It consist of Microbiology lab , Biochemistry lab , optometry lab , Histopathology lab , Dean office and Admin office .
