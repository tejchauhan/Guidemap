NEWTON BLOCK








Newton block is a high amphitheatre style block . It is situated in front of Edison block . It consists of hi-tech computer labs where all computers are connected to the internet through broad-band connection . It provides all the computer facilities comprising of the latest computer machines linked to a wide range of services .It is a high tech and fully A/C block with all the computational technologies and latest software to enable work on data analysis , web technologies . The internet provided enables close online interaction between faculty and students for online interaction . This block consists of three floors .

Ground floor - It consists of faculty rooms , Office of Vice President , Pro Vice - Chancellor office and computer labs . It also include Mac lab where apple technology lets the students and teachers unleash the creativity in them in their learning experiences .
First floor - It consisits of faculty room , computer labs and classrooms .
Second floor - It consists of computer labs and faculty room .
