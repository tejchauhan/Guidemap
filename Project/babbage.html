BABBAGE BLOCK






In order to achieve its ambition of being a high reputated university . It provides a highly professional faculty in chitkara University who work on the management and technical aspects of the university . Babbage block plays a vital role in all these fields . All the registrations, admissions, accounts and management are done over here .

Ground Floor - It includes Registrar office , Accounts office , Admission cell , it also consists of Chalkpad office , office of Talent Management and Conference room . On the backside it has Transport Office .
First floor - It consists of well build Examination cell , Dean office , Printing section where examination sheets and papers are printed . It also includes Evaluation room where the re-evaluations of answer sheets takes place .
Second floor - It includes high-tech build classrooms for Art and Design students . It consists of Da Vinci exhibition hall , Picaso junction and Conference hall .
Third floor - It includes Walt Disney classroom , Miyamoto classroom , Boratt classroom and Ogilvy classroom for art and design students .
Fourth floor - This floor is for interior designing students , it consists of Michelangelo lab for art design where students experience the various new art forms and Spielberg computer lab .
Fifth floor - It consists of Digital image processing lab and Electronics lab , AR/VR lab , liquid crystal nano physics lab , Research lab . CURIN department is also in this floor and has all the faculty rooms of Curin faculty .
Sixth floor - It consists of Office of International Program , Secretariat office and faculty rooms of Curin faculty .
