LIBRARY




Library services are provided at chitkara university . It has spacious reading halls and online database browsing areas .
It offers wide range of materials in variety of formats from traditional books , serial films , networked information from around the world . Highly skilled staff assists the students to find information on specific topics . Chitkara university inspires the students to explore , research and create their skills . Our library includes a collection of more than 50,000 Books .
Our University also provides number of e - resources online science direct etc . Apart from all these the students have access to many electronic journels available online and daily newspapers .
